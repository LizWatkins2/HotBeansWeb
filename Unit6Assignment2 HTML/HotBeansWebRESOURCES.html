Resources

At Hot Beans Web, we're committed to helping our team grow, learn, and succeed. Whether you're just starting out or looking to deepen your expertise, we've gathered a collection of valuable resources to support your journey in web development.

We encourage continuous learning. Here are some recommended platforms and tools:

Online coding platforms such as freeCodeCamp, Codecademy, and Coursera Documentation hubs like MDN Web Docs for in-depth technical references Version control tools like Git and GitHub to collaborate and manage projects

Great web development goes beyond code. Understanding design principles is key:

UI/UX fundamentals including accessibility and responsive design Tools like Figma, Adobe XD, or Sketch for prototyping Best practices for creating intuitive and user-friendly interfaces

Our team works with modern technologies, and we encourage familiarity with:

Front-end frameworks such as React, Vue, or Angular Back-end technologies like Node.js, Python, or PHP Databases including SQL and NoSQL solutions

Being part of a strong developer community can accelerate your growth:

Join developer forums, meetups, and hackathons Follow industry blogs, podcasts, and newsletters Contribute to open-source projects to build your portfolio

To help you succeed in our hiring process:

Practice coding challenges on platforms like LeetCode or HackerRank Review core concepts such as algorithms, data structures, and system design Prepare to discuss past projects and problem-solving approaches

We're looking for passionate, curious, and driven individuals who are excited about building exceptional web experiences. If you're ready to take the next step in your web development career, explore our open positions and apply today.
