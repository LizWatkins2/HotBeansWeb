About The Job

We're always on the lookout for talented, curious and driven individuals who are passionate about their work. Starting your career in web development is an exciting step, and at Hot Beans Web, we're here to help you build a strong foundation. As a Trainee Web Developer, you'll work alongside experienced developers, gaining hands-on experience in designing, building, and maintaining modern websites and applications. This role is designed for individuals who are passionate about technology and eager to learn. You'll be introduced to industry-standard tools, coding practices, and collaborative workflows, all while contributing to real projects that make an impact. From writing clean, efficient code to understanding user experience and performance, you'll develop the skills needed to grow into a confident and capable developer.

If you're curious, motivated, and ready to turn your interest in web development into a rewarding career, this is the perfect place to begin. We hope to see and hear from you soon!

Expected salary: ranging from £20,000 upwards to £30,000 on completion of the training.

Contracted hours: 40 hours a week.

Responsibilities of the job: hands- on work alongside the theoretical learning. You will deal with the front-end and the back-end development of the websites. This is giving you a full training for the whole course by learning the ins and outs of each process taking place. You will need to work with our team and clients to solve and find any solutions to the problems that are given by the customers.
